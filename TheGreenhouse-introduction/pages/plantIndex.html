PEPEROMIA

Peperomias are small plants withg fleshy leaves and stems. They make wonderful houseplants and can be found in both hanging and upright forms.

PEPEROMIA OBTUSIFOLIA

Light

Water

Difficulty

Also known as the baby rubber plant, this succulent like peperomia should be watered once or twice a week depending on light levels (water more often the brighter conditions). Similar to the peperomia it is recommended to wait for the soil to dry.
